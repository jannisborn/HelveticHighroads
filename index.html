Story

Mathis and Jannis ride to the highest paved road in every Swiss canton and leave a Helvetic Highroads sticker at each top.

This idea comes from a short film from the European Outdoor Film Tour (EOFT), where Tobi climbed the highest point of each canton and celebrated with a Gipfeli ritual.

Tobi's site: tobiasrenggli.ch/about.html

Rules

  • Ride throughout the year
  • Start each ride where the previous ride ended
  • Do not ride the same road twice
  • Do not cross your own track
  • Visit each neighboring country at least once: Germany, France, Italy, Austria, and Liechtenstein
  • Reach at least one canton peak on every ride
